🍪 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you interact with our platform, and improve your overall experience. Cookies are not harmful to your device and do not contain viruses or malware.

We use both session cookies (which expire when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which remain on your device for a set period).

📋 2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies on our platform:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the basic functionality of the platform, including authentication, security, and account management.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences, such as language selection, region, and login details.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our platform, allowing us to improve performance and user experience.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements and promotions based on your interests.
  • Performance Cookies: Monitor and optimize the technical performance of our platform.
📊 3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To keep you logged in securely during your session.
  • Preferences: To remember your language, currency, and display preferences.
  • Analytics: To analyze user behavior and improve our services.
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraudulent activity.
  • Personalization: To provide personalized content, promotions, and recommendations.
  • Performance: To ensure the platform loads quickly and runs smoothly.

🛠️ 6. Managing and Disabling Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View Cookies: See which cookies are stored on your device.
  • Delete Cookies: Remove existing cookies from your device.
  • Block Cookies: Prevent websites from storing cookies on your device.
  • Configure Exceptions: Allow cookies from specific websites while blocking others.

Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of our platform. You may not be able to log in, place bets, or access certain features.

To manage cookies in your browser, refer to the help section of your browser:

  • Firefox
  • Safari
  • Edge
