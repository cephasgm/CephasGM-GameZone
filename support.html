❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account? ▼
Creating an account is simple. Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner, fill in your details (email, password, and personal information), verify your email, and you're ready to start betting!
What payment methods are accepted? ▼
We accept a variety of payment methods including credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), bank transfers, mobile money (M-Pesa, Airtel Money, MTN), and cryptocurrencies (Bitcoin, Ethereum, USDT). All transactions are secure and encrypted.
How do I place a bet? ▼
To place a bet, navigate to the sports or event you're interested in, select the odds you want to bet on, enter your stake amount in the bet slip, and confirm your bet. Your bet will be placed instantly if you have sufficient funds.
How long do withdrawals take? ▼
Withdrawals are typically processed within 24-48 hours. Cryptocurrency withdrawals are usually faster (10-60 minutes). Bank transfers and card withdrawals may take 1-5 business days depending on your bank.
How do I verify my account? ▼
You can verify your account by going to the KYC section in your profile. You'll need to provide a government-issued ID (passport, driver's license) and proof of address (utility bill, bank statement). Verification usually takes 24-48 hours.
Why is the app not working? ▼
If the app is not working, try clearing your cache and cookies, updating the app to the latest version, or restarting your device. If the issue persists, check your internet connection or contact our support team for assistance.
What is cash out? ▼
Cash out allows you to settle your bet before the event ends. You can secure a portion of your potential winnings or minimize losses. The cash out amount is calculated based on current odds and is available on selected markets.
Is my personal information safe? ▼
Yes, we take security very seriously. All data is encrypted with 256-bit SSL encryption. We never share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent. We comply with all relevant data protection regulations.
